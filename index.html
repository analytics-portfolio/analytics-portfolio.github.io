Some more personal introduction...

I previously worked at Microsoft as a Data Science intern. I developed an unsupervised machine learning model to detect abnormal purchase events for Microsoft's global services. By implementing a near real-time monitoring system, my solution saved approximately $2-5 million in daily revenue from potentially failed purchase events.

As a side hustle, I'm building Business IQ Analytics (BIQA), an open-source business intelligence platform. BIQA empowers local businesses with data-driven insights from customer's feedback and a competitive edge in their local market. Local businesses can leverage BIQA to gain insights of their service quality, monitor their online presence through social listening metrics, and secure a competitive advantage by benchmarking their performance within their geographical area.

The BIQA platform aggregates data across various social platforms, including Yelp, TripAdvisor, OpenTable, Google, Instagram, and more, into a single dashboard. It then translate this data into valuable business metrics, providing actionable insights that drive business growth and success.

We've organized workshops on Machine Learning and brought in over 20 industry leaders from top S&P500 tech companies (Google, Microsoft, Nasdaq, Nike, and so on) to accelerate our community's career growth.
